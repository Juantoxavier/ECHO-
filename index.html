Who We Are

About FISAT ECHO

ECHO (Electronics and Communication Heuristic Organisation) is the premier student association of the ECE department at Federal Institute of Science and Technology (FISAT).

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academia and industry by organising hackathons, technical workshops, and mentorship programs that equip students with state-of-the-art technological skills.
